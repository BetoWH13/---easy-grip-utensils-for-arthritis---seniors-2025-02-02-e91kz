About Easy Grip Utensils

Founded in 2015 , Easy Grip Utensils began with a simple mission: to help people with arthritis and limited mobility maintain their independence and dignity. After witnessing her grandmother's struggles with standard utensils, Maria dedicated herself to creating innovative solutions that would make everyday tasks easier for those with hand mobility challenges.

Today, Easy Grip Utensils is committed to improving lives through thoughtful design and superior quality. Our core values of innovation, empathy, and excellence drive everything we do. We work closely with healthcare professionals and our customers to continuously improve our products and create solutions that make a real difference in people's daily lives.
